Motion Guideline

  • Head of Motion Design & ixD
  • 2014 to present
  • Product site wire.com

Wire is a simple tool to communicate with text, voice, pictures and media — all beautifully woven together for modern devices. A startup backed by Janus Friis, co-founder of Skype.

As Head of UI Motion Design I’m responsible to lead the creation of Wire's motion guideline. A reference document that synthesises the reasoning behind the transitions and behaviour of Wire user interface and provides a consistent language for the application along the different platforms and devices.

The guideline contains a numerous set of prototypes covering the most important interactions of the product. It’s conceived as a living document that will grow and update as the product continues to develop new features and explore new interactions.
